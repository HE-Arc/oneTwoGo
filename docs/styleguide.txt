# Colors
## Main colors
- dark grey : ```#272C38```
	- background
- darker grey : ```#1F2631```
	- boxes background
- even darker grey : ```#191D26```
	- to highlight selections in darker grey
- white : ```#FAFAFA```
	- for most of the font colors

## Singalisation colors
### Only use them when necessary
- Green : ```#27CF27```
- Red : ```#CF2727```
### When it's really really necessary
- Blue : ```#277BCF```
- Yellow : ```#CFCF27```
